Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account?

Click the "Get Started" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and verify your email. Your account will be created instantly!

Is BigStudy free to use?

Yes! Browsing colleges, courses, and exams is completely free. Premium features may be available in the future.

How accurate is the college information?

We source data from official NIRF rankings, college websites, and verified student reviews. However, we recommend contacting colleges directly for the most current information.

Can I apply to colleges through BigStudy?

BigStudy provides detailed college information and helps you decide. For applications, you'll be directed to official college portals or entrance exam websites.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link within minutes.

How can I delete my account?

Go to your account settings and click "Delete Account". Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
